About us

Bangladesh (officially the People's Republic of Bangladesh) is a country in South Asia. It is bordered by India to its west, north and east. Burma to its southeast and separated from Nepal and Bhutan by the Chicken's Neck corridor. To its south, it faces the Bay of Bengal. Bangladesh is a land embedded with a lot natural resources. It has a lot of rivers, canals and ponds as well as Ghers(for farming shrimps and prawns). Most of the people are concerned with agricultural work. The people concerned with aquatic products do farming with a great care as it is their the only earning source for living. The home government organizations and private sector people as well as so many foreign authorities work for the huge development and prosperity of the sector. Besides huge quantity of aquatic products particularly shrimps and prawns are found from the natural resources. And the seafood products of Bangladesh has a very good demand all over the world for its succulent taste and competitive price and easy availability as well.
